1. Draw Period and Repayment Terms

Your Home Equity Line of Credit features an initial draw period during which you may request advances against your credit line limit. Payments during the draw period are calculated based on interest accrued on outstanding balances or minimum specified flat percentages, subject to variable index movements tracking the Wall Street Journal Prime Rate.

2. Notice of Right to Cancel (3-Day Rescission)
NOTICE TO BORROWER: Under federal law and New York State codes, you have a statutory right to cancel this transaction without penalty within THREE (3) BUSINESS DAYS from the latest of: (a) the date the agreement is executed, (b) the date you receive your Truth-In-Lending disclosures, or (c) the date you receive this mandatory notice.

If you choose to rescind this agreement, you must submit a signed, dated written cancellation notice to the broker/lender before midnight of the third business day following transaction execution.
